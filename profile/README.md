### DAURINPOIN APPS
<img src="https://github.com/Capstone-DaurinPoin/.github/assets/100658838/1ec22da9-96bd-4f72-aff0-cf668cae2b8b" alt="Modern Download App Instagram Post" width="900" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;">

DaurinPoin application is a waste recycling application by providing incentives to users. The app allows users to filter their waste with their phone's camera and find out its type and point value. Users can also send and deliver their waste to designated places and earn points that can be exchanged for vouchers, coupons or donations. This application aims to help the community manage waste responsibly and sustainably.
